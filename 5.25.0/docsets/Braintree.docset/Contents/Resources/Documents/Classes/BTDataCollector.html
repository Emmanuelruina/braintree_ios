  • -collectDeviceData:

    Collects device data based on your merchant configuration.

    We recommend that you call this method as early as possible, e.g. at app launch. If that’s too early, calling it when the customer initiates checkout is also fine.

    Use the return value on your server, e.g. with Transaction.sale.
